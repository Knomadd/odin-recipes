HOW TO MAKE HAMBURGERS

This is just a short easy recipe to make every week for your whole family

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Cheese
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cook hamburger until slightly pink in the middle
  2. Add cheese and let it melt
  3. Place between buns
  4. Eat
