HOW TO MAKE STEAK

This is just a short easy recipe to make every week for your whole family

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Add lots of salt and pepper to cold steak and let sit at room temp
  2. Turn the flame on high and let cast iron pan heat
  3. Place steak on cast iron pan for 3 minutes each side
  4. Eat
