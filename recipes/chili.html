Chili

Delicous bowl of hearty chili

This is a delicous bowl of chili that's perfect for cold weather

  • Chili Beans
  • Hamburger
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chili Seasoning
  • Onions
  • Green Peppers
  • Cheese
  1. Brown the hamburger, while browning add the onions, green peppers and seasonings
  2. Once the hamburger is browned, add the beans and tomato sauce
  3. Bring the chili to a boil and let simmer for and hour
  4. Add cheese and serve
