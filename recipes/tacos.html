HOW TO MAKE TACOS

This is just a short easy recipe to make every week for your whole family

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Taco seasoning
  • cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Brown meat
  2. Drain grease
  3. Add seasoning
  4. Warm the shells
  5. Add meet, cheese, sour cream and salsa to the shell
  6. Eat
